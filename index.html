Far Gosford Street

Yesterday. Today. Tomorrow.

Far Gosford Street is one of Coventry’s most historic streets and has seen many changes throughout its long and colourful life. Across almost a thousand years of history, the street has seen both good times and bad. However one thing has remained the same throughout time – the street has always been a centre for creativity, innovation and enterprise.

The restoration and regeneration that has taken place over the last few years has started a new chapter in this cycle of change. The historic buildings are now used as the city’s creative quarter and home to many independent and unique businesses.

And there’s more to come, Fargo Village will open in 2013. Affordable rates will enable many artistic, creative and alternative businesses to set up and thrive in an attractive environment. Alongside the shopping will be a whole host of markets, cafes and entertainment, a destination for everyone to enjoy.
